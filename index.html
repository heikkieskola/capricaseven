Capricaseven

CRYSTAL CASTLES MEETS PROGRESSIVE HOUSE AND TRAP

Capricaseven, a visionary electronic artist and DJ hailing from the vibrant city of Helsinki, Finland, delves into the realms of melodic, progressive, and spiritual music. Embarking on his musical journey in December 2012, he became enamored with the electronic music world, inspiring him to craft a distinctive sound that seamlessly blends infectious melodies with introspective lyrics.

Capricaseven's sonic palette draws influences from a fusion of genres, including progressive house, trap, and metal, resulting in an underground yet accessible sound that captivates listeners.

With a focus on themes such as spirituality, spiritual growth, pain, and healing, Capricaseven's music transcends traditional boundaries, creating a profound and immersive experience for his audience.

Embark on a sonic adventure with Capricaseven's mesmerizing creations, available on popular platforms like Soundcloud, Spotify, and YouTube. Stay connected with the artist on Instagram for a deeper dive into his creative world.

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Capricaseven, where melody meets spirituality in a harmonious blend of electronic exploration.
